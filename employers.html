How it works

We are pleased to offer a cost-effective talent solution that works for your business. Employers simply pay per project post. You determine the lump sum contract payment that is suitable for your project demands and we’ll do the rest. We recommend a lump sum equal to a $20 - $25 hourly rate for your project. Please note that Plum retains 8% of your payment to cover administration fees.

Due to the short nature of the projects, we do not complete background checks on our students. We do however, complete a screening interview with every student that creates a profile. Students are required to sign an NDA before the project begins. Projects that require students to work with sensitive information or minors under the age of 18 are prohibited.

Please contact us or view out FAQ page if you have any more questions.
