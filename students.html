How it works

Once you create your profile and complete a screening interview with us, you have access to our project board and monthly newsletters. When you apply for a project, the business will be updated via email with your profile and resume. We will also recommend your profile to businesses and projects that align with your interests and experiences.

Projects are paid out in full after you complete a project through Square payroll. Please contact us or view our FAQ page if you have any more questions.
